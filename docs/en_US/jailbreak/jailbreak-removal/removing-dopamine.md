Uninstalling a jailbreak isn't as simple as deleting the app. When you uninstall Dopamine, you need to go in the app and restore parts of the OS to how they were before your device was jailbroken.

This guide is useful for people who just want to unjailbreak their device.

::: tip

If you're proceeding to installing palera1n (rootless), you do not need to follow this guide, for Dopamine and palera1n (rootless) are intercompatible with each other, and can both be used at the same time. palera1n (rootful) is not intercompatible.

:::

## Uninstalling

1. Open the Dopamine application
1. Tap on `Settings`
1. Press `Remove Jailbreak`
1. Press `Continue`
    - This may take some time
    - If it fails, try again from the beginning

Dopamine should now be uninstalled, leaving you safe to install any other jailbreak.
