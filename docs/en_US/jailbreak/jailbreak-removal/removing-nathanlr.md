Uninstalling a jailbreak isn't as simple as deleting the app. When you uninstall nathanlr, you need to go in the app and restore parts of the OS to how they were before your device was jailbroken.

This guide is useful for people who just want to unjailbreak their device.


## Uninstalling

1. Open the nathanlr application
1. Tap on `Settings`
1. Press `Remove Jailbreak`
1. Press `Yes`
    - This may take some time
    - If it fails, try again from the beginning

nathanlr should now be uninstalled, leaving you safe to install any other jailbreak.
